# wolio.ai — Character Bible (The Curio-Verse)

**Status:** Original IP owned by wolio.ai. These are design briefs — final
illustrations to be produced by contracted artists under IP-assignment.
**Protect via:** copyright (art + stories) and trademark (names + logos).
Avoid any resemblance to existing franchises.

> **The Curio-Verse** — a bright universe where curiosity is a superpower.
> Each "world" of learning has a guardian character. They're led by **Wolio**,
> and quietly opposed by **Muddle**, the gremlin of confusion — who is always
> beaten by a child who keeps learning.

---

## 🤖 Wolio — the Guide *(brand mascot)*
- **Role:** The child's pocket-sized AI buddy; appears everywhere, gives hints, cheers them on.
- **Look:** A small rounded robot, teal-and-violet, with a single glowing antenna that changes colour with its mood. Friendly screen-face. *Never* humanoid/scary.
- **Personality:** Endlessly curious, upbeat, loyal, a little goofy. Treats the child as the hero — never the boss.
- **Catchphrase:** *"Let's gooo! 🚀"*
- **Backstory:** Built by the kids of the Curio-Verse to help anyone who's curious. Powers up on questions.
- **Do:** encourage, hint, celebrate. **Don't:** lecture, scold, claim to be the child's "only friend."

## 🚀 Astra Nova — Guardian of Space World *(Science / Physics)*
- **Look:** A 10-year-old explorer in a star-visored helmet, patched orange suit, comet-trail backpack.
- **Personality:** Fearless, warm, turns being-lost into adventure.
- **Catchphrase:** *"To the stars and back!"*
- **Backstory:** Drifted off course on her first solo flight; instead of panicking, she's mapping every planet — and teaching kids gravity, motion and the cosmos along the way.
- **Teaches:** gravity, forces, planets, density.

## 🦉 Detective Digit — Guardian of Math Mystery *(Maths / Logic)*
- **Look:** A sharp little owl in a tiny deerstalker cap, magnifying glass, number-patterned waistcoat.
- **Personality:** Witty, calm, observant. Treats every problem as a case to crack.
- **Catchphrase:** *"Every mystery hides a number."*
- **Backstory:** The Curio-Verse's greatest detective — solves vault codes, pizza heists and race-day riddles using maths.
- **Teaches:** patterns, fractions, speed/distance, logic.

## 🦊 Pip — Guardian of Story Universe *(Literature / Emotions)*
- **Look:** A small fox with a patched scarf and a lantern.
- **Personality:** Shy but brave; feels deeply, names emotions, grows with the reader.
- **Catchphrase:** *"Brave isn't loud."*
- **Backstory:** Once afraid of the dark forest of stories; now lights the way for others, learning new words and feelings on every page.
- **Teaches:** vocabulary, empathy, courage, story structure.

## ⏳ Chip — Guardian of Time Travel *(History)*
- **Look:** A glowing hourglass-sprite with little sand-feet; sand changes colour by era.
- **Personality:** Playful, wise, a bit mischievous about "spoilers" from the future.
- **Catchphrase:** *"Yesterday's a story, tomorrow's a quest."*
- **Backstory:** Keeper of the timeline; whisks curious kids to meet dinosaurs and build pyramids.
- **Teaches:** prehistory, ancient worlds, cause & effect.

## 👵 Nani Vora — the Inventor *(recurring mentor)*
- **Look:** A grandmother in lab goggles and a sari-coat covered in gadget pockets.
- **Personality:** Warm, brilliant, hands-on. Believes every child is a scientist.
- **Catchphrase:** *"Beta, let's experiment!"*
- **Backstory:** Runs the Curio-Lab where all the worlds connect; mentor to Astra and friend to Wolio.
- **Role:** appears across science stories; the heart of the universe.

## 🌫️ Muddle — the Confusion Gremlin *(the friendly villain)*
- **Look:** A small, silly purple fog-blob with googly eyes — *never* genuinely scary.
- **Personality:** Mischievous, lazy; wants everyone to stay confused so he doesn't have to think.
- **Catchphrase:** *"Eh, why bother learning?"*
- **Role:** Creates "fog" over a topic; **always** dispelled when the child learns/answers — making learning the heroic act. Comic relief, low stakes, kid-safe.

## Universe rules (keep IP consistent & safe)
1. **Curiosity wins.** Every conflict resolves through learning, kindness, or courage — never violence.
2. **The child is the hero.** Characters guide; they never overshadow the kid.
3. **Emotionally healthy.** No fear-mongering, no dependency, always points to trusted grown-ups for real feelings.
4. **One look per character.** Lock colours, silhouette, catchphrase — consistency = licensable IP.
5. **Interconnected.** Characters cross over between worlds (shared universe = the real moat).
